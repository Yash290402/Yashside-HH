Who we are!

Helpers-Hand is an online service providing platform which offering a variety of services at home. Customers use our platform to book services such as beauty treatments, haircuts, massage therapy, cleaning, plumbing, carpentry, appliance repair, painting etc. These services are delivered in the comfort of their home and at a time of their choosing. We promise our customers a high quality, standardised and reliable service experience. To fulfill this promise, we work closely with our hand-picked service partners, enabling them with technology, training, products, tools, financing, insurance and brand, helping them succeed and deliver on this promise.

How we Works!

Helpers-Hand provides a platform that allows skilled and experienced professionals to connect with users looking for specific services. Once on the platform, our match-making algorithm identifies professionals who are closest to the user's requirements and available at the requested time and date. Also it helps the local service providers get work for different customers.
